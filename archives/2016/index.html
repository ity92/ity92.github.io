Introduction to hand tracking

2016-05-17
  • blog

In the field of technology and image processing, hand/finger tracking is a high-resolution technique that is employed to know the consecutive position of the hands/fingers of the user and hence represent objects in 3D.

In addition to that, the hand/finger tracking technique is used as a tool of the computer,acting as an external device in our computer, similar to a keyboard and a mouse.
